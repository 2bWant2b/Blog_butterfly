# 描述现象作文

这个模板的中文大意是：在某种场合，发生某种现象，并提供一些相关数据，然后列出这种现象的三个原因，并将三个原因总结为一个最主要原因，最后提出避免这种现象的两个办法。

   Nowadays, there are more and more [某种现象] in [某种场合]. It is estimated that [相关数据]. （描述现象）

  Why have there been so many [某种现象]? It is not easy to identify the reason for… which involve several complicated factors.Maybe the reasons can be listed as follows. The first one is [原因一]. Besides, [原因二]. The third one is [原因三]. To sum up, the main cause of [某种现象] is due to [最主要原因]. （分析原因）

  It is high time that something were done upon it. The first key factor to solve this problem is, [解决办法一]. Another key factor is, [解决办法二]. All these measures will certainly reduce the number of [某种现象]. （解决方法）

# 说明利弊型作文

这种题型往往要求先说明一下现状，再对比事物本身的利弊，有时也会单从一个角度 (利或弊) 出发，最后往往要求考生表明自己的态度 (或对事物前景提出预测)。

  Nowadays many people prefer A because it has a significant role in our daily life. Generally, its advantages can be seen as follows. First ----------------(A 的优点之一). Besides -------------------(A 的优点之二). （阐述优点）

  But every coin has two sides. The negative aspects are also apparent. One of the important disadvantages is that ----------------(A 的第一个缺点).To make matters worse,------------------(A 的第二个缺点). （阐述缺点）

  Through the above analysis, I believe that the positive aspects overweigh the negative ones. Therefore, I would like to ---------------(我的看法).

  From the comparison between these positive and negative effects of A, we should take it reasonably and do it according to the circumstances we are in. Only by this way, ---------------(对前景的预测).

# 对比观点型（选择型）作文

要求论述两个对立的观点或选择其一并给出自己的看法。

  The topic of ①-----------------(主题) is becoming more and more popular recently. There are two sides of opinions of it. (In fact,both have their own advantages,and should be put into perspective.) Some people say A is their favorite. They hold their view for the reason of ②-----------------(支持 A 的理由一) What is more, ③------------- 理由二). Moreover, ④---------------(理由三).

  While others think that B is a better choice in the following three reasons. Firstly,-----------------(支持 B 的理由一). Secondly (besides),⑥------------------(理由二). Thirdly (finally),⑦------------------(理由三).

  From my point of view, I think ⑧----------------(我的观点). The reason is that ⑨--------------------(原因). As a matter of fact, there are some other reasons to explain my choice. For me, the former is surely a wise choice .

# 解决问题型作文

要求考生列举出解决问题的多种途径 1. 问题现状 2. 怎样解决 (解决方案的优缺点)

  In recent days, we have to face I problem-----A, which is becoming more and more serious. First, ------------(说明 A 的现状).Second, ---------------(举例进一步说明现状)

  Confronted with A, we should take a series of effective measures to cope with the situation. For one thing, ---------------(解决方法一). For another -------------(解决方法二). Finally, --------------(解决方法三).

  Personally, I believe that -------------(我的解决方法). Consequently, I’m confident that a bright future is awaiting us because --------------(带来的好处).
